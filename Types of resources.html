Renewable and Non-Renewable Resources

Renewable Resources

Those natural resources that will never run out or can be replaced within a reasonable period of time through natural
processes. These are further categorised as Exhaustible and InExhaustible Resources. For example, INEXHAUSTIBLE RESOURCES
are Sunlight, Water, Air & EXHAUSTIBLE RESOURCES are soil Forests, Groundwater.

Non-Resources

Those natural resources that are once used up cannot be replaced within a reasonable period of time. These are further categorised as the one which Can Be Recycled and others which Cannot Be Recycled. Examples of CAN BE RECYCLED are Minerals & that of CANNOT BE RECYLED are TopSoil, Fossil Fuels.
